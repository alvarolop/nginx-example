Did You Know?

The concept of cloud computing dates back to the 1960s when computer scientist John McCarthy suggested that computing could someday be organized as a public utility, much like water or electricity. However, it wasn't until the early 2000s, with the advent of services like Amazon Web Services (AWS), that cloud computing truly began to take off, revolutionizing the way we store and process data.
