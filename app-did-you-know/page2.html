Did You Know?

OpenShift, Red Hat's Kubernetes-based platform, was initially released in 2011. It started as a PaaS (Platform as a Service) before evolving into the powerful container orchestration platform it is today. OpenShift is often praised for its developer-friendly tools and enterprise-grade security features.
